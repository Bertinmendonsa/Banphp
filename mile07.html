2007

  • Started by Dr. N Veerabhadra Rao, Vitreo-Retinal Surgeon and Dr. P. Sharada Rao, a Glaucoma & Refractive Surgeon.
  • Major Motto “THE BEST EYE CARE TO MANKIND”.
  • Started with 2 Bed hospital in Banashankari II Stage, Bangalore.
  • FFA, Green Lasers & Operation Theatre equipped with Phaco & Vitrectomy Machine.
  • Started Sharada Optical wing in the same premises.
  • Inaugurated by then Honorable Union Minister Sri M. V. Rajashekharan, Dr. Kim Ramasamy(Retina Chief at Aravind Eye Hospital, Madurai) and Dr. Usha Kim(Chief, Orbit & Oculoplasty at Aravind Eye Hospital, Madurai).
